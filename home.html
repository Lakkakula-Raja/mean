The GATE examination consists of a signal online paper of 3-hour duration.
	In which there will be a total of 65 Questions carrying 100 marks.
	Gate Exam  will be conducted by 7 IIT's and IISc .
		
Note:-
	For 1-mark Questions, 1/3 mark will be deducted for Multiple Choice Questions.
	For 2-mark Questions, 2/3 mark will be deducted for Multiple Choice Questions.
	There is NO Negative marking for a Numerical Answer Type Questions 
	and as well as Multiple selective Questions.
